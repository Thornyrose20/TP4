Seven Layer Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,melted
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened coca powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • 9" x 13" baking dish
  • Measuring cups
  • whisk
  • Sifter
  • large bowl
  • Wire Rack

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Place butter in 13" x 9" inch pan and melt in oven. Swirl to coat bottom and sides with butter.
  3. Spread graham cracker crumbs evenly over bottom of pan.
  4. Layer chocolate chips, butterscotch chips, and nuts over crumbs.
  5. Pour condensed milk over nuts.
  6. Sprinkle coconut over condensed milk.
  7. Bake until edges are golden brown, about 25 minutes.
  8. Let cool before cutting into bars.
